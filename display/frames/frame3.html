Mbappé was born in Paris[5] and was raised in Bondy, Seine-Saint-Denis, a commune 10.9 km (6.8 mi) from the centre of Paris.[6] His father, Wilfried, is from Cameroon, and, as well as being Mbappé's agent, is a football coach, while his mother, Fayza Lamari, is of Algerian (Kabyle) origin and is a former handball player.[7][8] He has a younger brother, Ethan, who played for PSG's under-12 squad in 2018.[9][10] Mbappé's adoptive brother, Jirès Kembo Ekoko, is also a professional football player.[11] Growing up, his idol was Cristiano Ronaldo, a player he looked to emulate.
